01 Product Copywriting

Copy that sells
the feeling,
not the feature.

Customers don't buy specifications. They buy outcomes, identity, and trust.

Most product descriptions read like warehouse inventory — size, material, color, done. But that's not how people decide to buy. They decide because something made them feel understood, or inspired, or certain that this specific product was made for them.

We rewrite your product pages to speak to what your customers actually want — in your brand voice, matched exactly to how you'd say it at your best.

Before

"Handmade soy wax candle. Burns for 45 hours. Available in 3 scents. Made with natural ingredients. Great for gifts."

After

"Some rooms just feel different. This hand-poured soy candle — 45 hours of slow, clean burn — is the reason. Choose your scent, light it once, and you'll understand why customers keep coming back for more."

– Product page rewrites
– Homepage & category copy
– Collection page descriptions
– Brand voice guide included
02 Email Campaigns

Emails people
actually
open.

Your email list is the most valuable asset your business owns — most businesses just don't know how to use it.

The average cold email gets deleted in 3 seconds. The average promotional email never gets opened. Why? Because most email copy sounds like it was written for nobody in particular — generic subject lines, boilerplate openers, CTAs that don't make the reader feel anything.

We write emails that feel like they came from a person, not a company. Sequences that guide subscribers from stranger to loyal buyer, one message at a time.

Subject Line Example

You left something behind (and we kept it warm)

Hey [Name],

You were so close. The [Product] is still in your cart — we're holding it for 24 more hours.

If something stopped you — price, a question, just not the right moment — reply to this email and we'll sort it out personally.

✦ Personal tone · Creates urgency · Opens a real conversation

– Welcome sequences (5 emails)
– Promotional campaigns
– Abandoned cart flows
– Re-engagement campaigns
